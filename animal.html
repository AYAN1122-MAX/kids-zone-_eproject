Animal Gallery



Lion
Lions are big, powerful cats often called the "king of the jungle."
Elephant
Elephants are the largest land animals on earth! They have a huge body, long trunks, big ears, and wrinkly grey or brown skin.
Giraffe
Giraffes are tall, gentle animals with very long necks and legs, making them the tallest living land animals on Earth.
Tiger
A tiger is a big cat with orange fur and black stripes. It's the largest cat in the world and a powerful hunter.
Penguin
Penguins are famous for their waddling walk and are excellent swimmers, using their flippers (short, paddle-like wings) to move through the water.
Dolphin
Dolphins are amazing sea mammals known for their intelligence and playful nature.
Cat
Cats are furry, four-legged mammals known for their playful and cuddly nature. They come in many sizes, colors, and coat lengths, and are popular pets for kids
Dog
Dogs are furry, four-legged mammals known as "man's best friend". They are often playful and loyal companions, and come in many different sizes, shapes, and colors
Butterfly
Butterflies are beautiful flying insects with colorful, patterned wings. They have six legs, two antennae, and three body parts: a head, thorax, and abdomen.
Peacock
Peacocks are big, beautiful birds, especially the males, with long, colorful tail feathers that they can fan out like a colorful fan.
